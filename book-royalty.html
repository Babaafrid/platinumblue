Book Royalty

Authors who publish books with Platinum Blue Publishing Corporation are entitled to receive royalties based on the sales of their books. Royalties are typically calculated as a percentage of net revenue generated from book sales, with authors receiving a share of the proceeds.

Our royalty rates are competitive and vary depending on factors such as the format of the book (e.g., print, ebook), sales channels, and distribution arrangements. Authors will receive detailed information about royalty rates and payment terms upon acceptance of their book proposal.

We are committed to providing authors with fair compensation for their creative and intellectual contributions. Our royalty payments are processed promptly and transparently, ensuring that authors receive their rightful earnings in a timely manner.
