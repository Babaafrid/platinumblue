Submission Guidelines for Authors

Thank you for considering submitting your work to Platinum Blue Publishing Corporation. We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of quality and integrity in scholarly publishing. Please carefully review the following guidelines before submitting your paper or book manuscript for consideration.

  • Manuscript Preparation

    • Check that your manuscript is well-written, logically organized, and free of typographical errors and grammatical mistakes.Include all necessary components such as title page, abstract, keywords, main text, references, figures, tables, and appendices, as applicable.

  • Submission Process

    • Submit your manuscript electronically through our online submission system, which can be accessed via our website. If you encounter any difficulties with the submission process, please contact our editorial team for assistance.

  • Peer Review Process

    • Upon receipt of your submission, it will undergo a rigorous peer review process. Expert reviewers in your field will evaluate the quality, originality, and significance of your work.

  • Revision and Resubmission

    • If your manuscript requires revisions based on feedback from peer reviewers, you will have the opportunity to revise and resubmit your work. Make revisions carefully and thoroughly, addressing all comments and suggestions from reviewers. Include a detailed response letter outlining the changes made in response to reviewer feedback.

  • Acceptance and Publication

    • Upon acceptance for publication, your manuscript will undergo final preparation for publication, including copyediting, typesetting, and formatting.

  • Copyright and Licensing

    • Be aware of the copyright and licensing terms associated with your publication. You will be required to sign a publishing agreement granting us the right to publish and distribute your work.
